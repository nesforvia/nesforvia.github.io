Imperial information

About the Imperial Kingdoms of Nesforvia

The Imperial Kingdoms of Nesforvia is an environmentalist two-leader empire led by Emperor Doom and Emperor Max I. It was formed through the unification of Garforvia and Nesfield, and now works with three member states: the Starian-Trosso Union, the Republic of Pinland, and the Great Velkar Union.

National overview

The Nesforvian flag is mainly green and carries the White Rose of Nesforvia. The green theme represents the empire's environmentalist direction, while the white rose acts as the central national symbol.

Nesforvia is also setting up a new province called The Republic of Serarin (ROS). Its purpose is to oppose the feud with the United Seranese Peoples Republics (USPR), described by Nesforvia as a dictatorship that censors its public.

Member states

The Republic of Pinland

Status: IKN member state

Pinland is close to Liverpool and is described as a strong micronation with a libertarian ideology. Its flag uses the White Rose of Nesforvia, a star above it, and blue, black and yellow as its main colours.

Leader: @theamazingperson7646 (Elijah)

Starian-Trosso Union (S-TU)

Status: IKN member state

The S-TU is made from two unified micronations: Staria and Trosso. Both are imperial. Its flag has two white triangles facing inward towards a central circle, wreath, star, and dark blue as the main colour.

Leader: @jake663723 (ʙᴏx.)

Great Velkar Union (GVU)

Status: IKN member state

The GVU was previously a dictatorship, but it is being revamped to fit the style and direction of the Imperial Kingdoms of Nesforvia. Its flag uses purple and black with a Nordic-style shape.

Leader: @thegreatvelkarunion (Leader of Velkar)

Nesforvian provinces

Garforvia

Garforvia is a Nesforvian province and was once a micronation. It unified with Nesfield to create Nesforvia. It is located in Altofts, West Yorkshire, near Wakefield and Leeds, and borders Normanton.

Ran by: @max_d_evans (Emperor Max I)

Nesfield

Nesfield is a Nesforvian province. It had not previously operated as a micronation, but unified with Garforvia to create Nesforvia. It is located in Belle Isle, Leeds.

Ran by: @.oliver2025 (Emperor Doom)
